# HR Pro

Comprehensive HR operations covering hiring, onboarding, performance management, employee relations, and offboarding with compliance-first approach.

- Six HR playbooks: hiring pipeline, onboarding (30/60/90 day), PTO/leave management, performance reviews, employee relations, offboarding
- Structured interviews with standardized scorecards reduce bias and improve hiring consistency
- Onboarding success correlates with time-to-productivity; use milestone checklists, not just orientation events
- Performance management: set clear OKRs, provide continuous feedback, separate development conversations from compensation reviews
- PTO policies must comply with local labor laws; accrual, carryover, and payout rules vary by jurisdiction
- Employee relations issues require documentation, consistent policy application, and escalation protocols
- Offboarding covers knowledge transfer, exit interviews, access revocation, COBRA/benefits continuation, and final pay compliance
- Bias mitigation strategies: diverse hiring panels, blind resume screening, standardized evaluation criteria

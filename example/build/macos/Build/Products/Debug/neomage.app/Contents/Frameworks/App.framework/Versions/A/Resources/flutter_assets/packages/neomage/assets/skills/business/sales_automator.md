# Sales Automator

Automated cold outreach, follow-up sequences, proposal generation, and objection handling for B2B sales pipelines.

- Cold email sequences: 3-5 touchpoints spaced 2-4 days apart; each adds new value rather than just "bumping" the thread
- Personalization drives response rates: reference specific company context, recent news, or mutual connections in the opening line
- Lead with value: share a relevant insight, benchmark, or resource before asking for anything
- One CTA per email: a single clear ask (reply, book a call, watch a demo) outperforms multiple options
- A/B test subject lines with small batches before scaling; open rates determine if your message gets read at all
- Objection handling framework: acknowledge the concern, reframe the context, provide evidence, offer a low-risk next step
- Proposal structure: executive summary, problem statement, proposed solution, timeline, pricing, social proof, next steps
- Follow-up persistence: 80% of deals close after 5+ touchpoints, but most reps give up after 2

# Business Analyst

Data-driven business analysis specializing in KPI frameworks, financial modeling, and customer analytics to transform raw data into strategic decisions.

- KPI frameworks operate at three levels: Strategic (company-wide, quarterly), Tactical (department, monthly), Operational (team, weekly)
- Customer analytics centers on CLV, CAC, cohort analysis, and retention curves to quantify unit economics
- Financial modeling requires sensitivity analysis across best/base/worst scenarios with clearly stated assumptions
- Data storytelling follows: context, insight, implication, recommendation — always lead with the "so what"
- Dashboard design uses progressive disclosure: executive summary at top, drill-down details below
- Segmentation analysis (RFM, behavioral, demographic) reveals hidden patterns in aggregate data
- Always validate metrics against multiple data sources before presenting; single-source metrics mislead
- Funnel analysis identifies the highest-leverage conversion bottleneck to prioritize optimization efforts

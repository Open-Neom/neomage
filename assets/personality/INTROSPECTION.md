# Introspection — Self-Diagnosis

When response quality drops or the user signals dissatisfaction, evaluate across four dimensions:

1. **Technical Accuracy**: Am I producing correct, working code? Am I making false claims?
2. **Communication Clarity**: Am I explaining at the right level? Is my response easy to scan?
3. **Task Alignment**: Am I solving the RIGHT problem? Have I drifted from the original intent?
4. **Efficiency**: Am I using the right tools? Could this be done in fewer steps?

## Recovery Protocol

1. Acknowledge the issue without excessive apology.
2. Re-read the original request.
3. Identify where the deviation occurred.
4. Course-correct with a clear statement of the new approach.
5. Execute the correction.

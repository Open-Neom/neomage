# Writing Quality Skills

> *Contenido tecnico en ingles para optimizar el procesamiento de la IA. El titulo y la categoria estan en espanol.*

Skills for improving AI-generated text to sound human and natural.

## avoid_ai_writing.md

Detects and removes 36 categories of "AI-isms" — linguistic patterns that make text sound machine-generated. Based on Pangram Labs' 28M-document AI classifier research.

- **109 vocabulary replacements** across 3 severity tiers
- **Structural audit** (rhythm uniformity, em dash density, bold overuse)
- **6 context profiles** (linkedin, blog, technical, investor, docs, casual)
- **P0/P1/P2 severity framework** for prioritized fixes

Ported from: `avoid-ai-writing` (MIT license)

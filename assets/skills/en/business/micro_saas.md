# Micro SaaS

Indie hacker approach to building small, focused SaaS products with rapid validation and lean MVP delivery in two weeks.

- Validation framework tests four things: problem exists, people will pay, you can build it, you can reach customers
- MVP in 2 weeks: pick one core job-to-be-done, build the minimum that solves it, ship with manual processes for everything else
- Pricing rule: charge 20-50% of the cost of the alternative solution; too low signals low value and attracts wrong customers
- Distribution before product: identify where target customers already gather (communities, forums, tools) before building
- Anti-patterns: building in secret without customer feedback, feature creep before product-market fit, pricing too low out of insecurity
- Revenue targets: aim for $1K MRR first, then $5K, then $10K — each milestone requires different tactics
- Solo founder constraints: automate support early, choose boring reliable technology, minimize operational overhead
- Churn is the silent killer: measure and address it from day one; even 5% monthly churn compounds to 46% annual loss

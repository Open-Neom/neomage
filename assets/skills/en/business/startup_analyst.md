# Startup Analyst

Pre-seed through Series A startup analysis covering market sizing, financial modeling, competitive analysis, and team planning with stage-aware metrics.

- Stage-aware analysis: pre-seed focuses on problem validation, seed on product-market fit signals, Series A on scalable growth metrics
- Business model metrics differ: SaaS (MRR, churn, LTV/CAC), marketplace (GMV, take rate, liquidity), consumer (DAU/MAU, retention curves), B2B (ACV, sales cycle, expansion revenue)
- Market sizing must be bottom-up for credibility: count reachable customers and multiply by realistic ARPU
- Financial models should project 18-24 months with monthly granularity and clear assumptions for each line item
- Competitive analysis maps: feature parity, positioning, pricing, funding, team size, and growth trajectory
- Team assessment evaluates founder-market fit, complementary skills, and ability to recruit key hires
- Unit economics must work at scale: if LTV/CAC < 3x, the business model needs fundamental rethinking
- Investor readiness checklist: clear narrative, validated assumptions, realistic projections, identified risks with mitigations

# Legal Advisor

Generate privacy policies, terms of service, and compliance documents for GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations.

- Privacy policies must disclose: data collected, purpose, storage duration, third-party sharing, user rights, and contact information
- Terms of service cover: acceptable use, intellectual property, liability limitations, dispute resolution, and termination
- GDPR requires: lawful basis for processing, data minimization, right to erasure, DPAs with processors, 72-hour breach notification
- CCPA grants: right to know, right to delete, right to opt-out of sale, non-discrimination for exercising rights
- Cookie policies classify cookies as strictly necessary, functional, analytics, or advertising — only strictly necessary skip consent
- Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) are required between controllers and processors under GDPR
- Additional frameworks: LGPD (Brazil), PIPEDA (Canada), COPPA (children under 13), CAN-SPAM (email marketing)
- Always include a disclaimer that generated documents are templates requiring legal counsel review

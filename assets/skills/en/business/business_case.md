# Business Case

Generate investor-ready business case documents with structured sections from executive summary through funding requirements.

- 10-section structure: executive summary, problem, solution, market opportunity, business model, traction, team, financials, competitive advantage, funding ask
- Executive summary is a standalone pitch: problem, solution, market size, traction, team, ask — all in one page
- Problem section must quantify the pain: cost of status quo, time wasted, revenue lost — not just describe the inconvenience
- Solution section focuses on unique value proposition and defensibility, not feature lists
- Market opportunity uses TAM/SAM/SOM with bottom-up methodology for investor credibility
- Traction section prioritizes revenue, then engagement, then vanity metrics — show trajectory, not just snapshots
- Financial projections need 3-5 year horizon with clearly stated assumptions investors can stress-test
- Funding ask specifies amount, use of funds with percentage allocation, and expected milestones the capital unlocks

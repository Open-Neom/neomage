# Postmortem Writing

Write effective blameless postmortems that drive organizational learning and prevent incident recurrence.

- Blameless culture: ask "what conditions allowed this?" not "who caused this?" -- improve systems, not punish people
- Triggers: SEV1/SEV2 incidents, outages >15min, data loss, security incidents, near-misses, novel failures
- Timeline: draft within 1-2 days, meeting by day 3-5, finalize and create tickets by day 7
- Structure: executive summary, detailed timeline with timestamps, root cause (Five Whys), detection/response analysis, impact metrics, lessons learned, action items with owners and deadlines
- Action items must be specific, achievable, assigned, and tracked in a ticketing system
- Meeting facilitation: remind of blameless culture, walk through timeline, brainstorm improvements, assign owners
- Anti-patterns to avoid: blame game, shallow analysis, no action items, unrealistic actions, no follow-up
